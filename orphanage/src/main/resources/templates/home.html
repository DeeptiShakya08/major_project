Education!

A life changing Oportunity

For many who live in countries stricken by poverty, even basic literacy is out of reach, leaving many to work unskilled, often dangerous jobs, for pay too small to care for themselves or their families. These circumstances create conditions that leave children vulnerable, often destitute, placed in the care of others or worse, alone. Providing a child with even a basic education will allow them to pursue better jobs, creating future opportunities for themselves, and their future families. Providing an education is a critical step toward breaking the cycle that creates orphans.
